About Radiance Games Studio

Radiance Games Studio is a small indie team creating games that are fast, focused, and emotionally meaningful. Ukrainian Birds is our first title – an arcade experience built to support and celebrate Ukrainian resilience.

  • • Simple controls – tap to fly, tap to fire.
  • • Earn coins and upgrade your weapons in the in-game shop.
  • • Ground & air enemies across stylised Ukrainian landscapes.
