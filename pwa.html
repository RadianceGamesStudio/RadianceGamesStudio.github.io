Ukrainian Birds – Web App

Play in the browser or install it like a native app.

Install on your device

On Android / desktop (Chrome, Edge):
Open this page in your browser, then use the menu (⋮ or ⋯) and choose “Install app” or “Install Ukrainian Birds”.
Chrome Edge Brave
On iPhone / iPad (Safari):
Tap the ⤴︎ share button, then “Add to Home Screen”.
Safari only
Once installed, launching the app from your home screen will jump directly into the game.
Tip: install it once, then play even more like a native game.
When opened from the home screen, the app launches the full game automatically.
